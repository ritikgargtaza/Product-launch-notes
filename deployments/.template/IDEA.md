# [Product / Feature Name]

Replace this with your early idea. This does not need to be a full PRD — a one-pager is enough.

## What is this?
What are we trying to build? 1–3 sentences on the core idea.

## Why are we building it?
What problem does it solve? Who asked for it?

## Who is it for?
Target customer, merchant type, or segment. Which geographies?

## How does it roughly work?
High-level flow — no technical spec needed, just the concept.

## What do we think changes?
Any early thoughts on what this touches — compliance, payments, legal, licensing, etc.

## Known constraints or risks
Anything you already know could be a blocker or complication.

## Open questions
What don't we know yet that someone needs to answer before we can scope this properly?
