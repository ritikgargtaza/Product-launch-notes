# Product Requirements Document Template

Replace this with your actual PRD. Include:

## Overview
- What is the feature/product?
- Why are we launching it?
- Who is the target customer/segment?

## Scope
- What functionality is included?
- What is explicitly out of scope?

## Key Changes
- What is new or different from the current state?
- What integrations or dependencies are affected?

## Impact Areas
- **Geographies**: Which countries/regions?
- **Volumes**: Expected transaction volumes?
- **Pricing/Revenue**: How do customers pay? Revenue impact?
- **Customer Segments**: Who uses this? (merchants, enterprises, etc.)

## Timeline
- When is launch?
- Any phased rollout or pilot?

## Technical Details
- New payment rails or schemes?
- API changes or new endpoints?
- Data flows or schema changes?
- Third-party integrations?

## Risk/Compliance Notes
- Any regulatory considerations?
- New data types or processing activities?
- Customer segment changes that affect AML/KYC?
- Currency or cross-border implications?
