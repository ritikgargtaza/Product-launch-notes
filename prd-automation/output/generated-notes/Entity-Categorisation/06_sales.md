# Sales — Release Notes: Entity Categorisation

## What's new

We're adding **merchant risk categorisation** to our compliance toolkit. Compliance can now instantly categorise any OBO merchant as low-risk (Category A), medium-risk (Category B), or high-risk (Category C) — and our system enforces hard stops for B and C until compliance approves. We're also capturing **service provider type** (are they a payment processor or a tech platform?) at onboarding, giving us crisp signals we used to lose in call notes.

## What's in it for you

**This unblocks "Category A onboarding" deals.** Prospects asking "Can you give us OBO payouts with auto-approval?" now get a clear answer: "Yes, if you're low-risk (Category A)." Merchants in higher-risk verticals still get approved quickly, but with a compliance gate that satisfies our regulators and risk team.

**Merchant type visibility:** You now know upfront if a prospect is a payment processor (PSP) or just using our platform (TSP). PSPs are higher-value but higher-touch deals. TSPs are faster, higher-volume opportunities. Sales can position accordingly.

**Removes a friction point:** No more "we'll need 2 weeks to set up your compliance rules" after deal close. Categorisation happens on day 1, and you can tell the customer the approval timeline instantly.
